Retro Pig Game

Pig (dice game): This is a popular and simple dice game where players take turns rolling a single die (or two dice, depending on the variation). At each turn, you can choose to either: Roll again: Keep adding the new roll to your current turn score. You can keep rolling as long as you don't roll a 1. Hold: Stop rolling and add your current turn score to your overall score. The turn then passes to the next player. If you roll a 1, your turn score goes to 0 and it becomes the other player's turn. The first player to reach a certain target score (often 100) wins the game.
